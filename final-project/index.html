Educate. Empower. Achieve.

At the “I Have A Dream” Foundation Boulder County, we believe that when given equal access to the resources they need to succeed, all children can ignite their innate potential and achieve their dreams. We partner with youth and their families as they successfully navigate school, college, and career by providing long-term, holistic academic, social, and emotional programming for our Dreamer Scholars – from 1st grade through college – and supporting Dreamer Scholars with postsecondary scholarships.

The Numbers

3x

Dreamers are over  3x more likely to earn a bachelor’s degree  than their peers who experience income inequality.

91%

91% of Dreamers  graduate High School,  compared to 74% of  students experiencing income inequality  nationally.

$1M

College graduates  earn, on average,  an additional $1 Million  in lifetime earnings.
